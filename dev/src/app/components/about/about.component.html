About Maggie

Magdalena Luna is an up-and-coming Tattoo Artist with two years of experience. After completing an apprenticeship in Logan, Utah, she began touring the country tattooing at expos, drawing orignal artwork, and guest spotting at local shops. In her free time she enjoys collaborating art in many different mediums, traveling, and spending time with her son. Take a look at her instagram for updates and where she'll be next!
